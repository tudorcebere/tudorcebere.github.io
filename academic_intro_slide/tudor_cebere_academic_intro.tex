\begin{block}{Research Interests}
        \fontsize{6.9}{7.8}\selectfont
        \begin{itemize}
          \setlength\itemsep{0.12em}

          \item \textbf{Privacy auditing.}
          I make privacy audits realistic and actionable:
          fully observational auditing
          \href{https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.14591}{\textcolor{InriaRed}{[1]}},
          practical DP-SGD threat models~\textcolor{InriaRed}{[2]},
          and tools for real DP libraries~\textcolor{InriaRed}{[3]}.
          My longer-term goal is to facilitate measuring privacy lower bounds across algorithms,
          settings, making risk measurable and communicable, particularly for large models.

          \item \textbf{Private learning in practice.}
          I study DP learning under realistic deployment constraints, including
          varied threat models~\textcolor{InriaRed}{[2]} and constrained
          learning settings~\textcolor{InriaRed}{[4]}.

          \item \textbf{Current interest.}
          I am aiming a) continue to push the limits of what we can do with privacy auditing (particularly for machine learning) and b) seeking for alternatives for differentially private machine learning beyond DP-SGD.
        \end{itemize}
      \end{block}
